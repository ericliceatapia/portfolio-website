My name is Eric Licea Tapia. I was born in 2004 in Dallas, Texas. I am a first generation Mexican-American college student currently studying at the Texas Christian University, pursuing a degree in Computer Science.

I am the oldest of three children to immigrant parents, who gave up everything to provide a better future for us. I elevate their stories because it is their sacrifice that brought me here. My family is from a small town outside of San Miguel de Allende, Guanajuato, Mexico, called Los Rodriguez.
